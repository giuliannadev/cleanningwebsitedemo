About Us

Learn more about our company, our mission, and the team that makes it all happen.

Our Story

CleanSweep was founded in 2015 by Sarah Johnson with a vision to provide exceptional cleaning services with a personal touch. Starting with a small team and a commitment to quality, we've grown into a trusted name in the cleaning industry, serving both residential and commercial clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create clean, healthy, and welcoming environments for our clients. We strive to exceed expectations with every cleaning, using eco-friendly products and meticulous attention to detail.

Our Values

At CleanSweep, we are guided by our core values: integrity, reliability, and excellence. We believe in honesty, transparency, and delivering consistent, high-quality service.
